ABOUT THE GAME
In LIGHT, you are Operator 07, the last sentinel in an isolated relay bunker. Your mission is simple, but deadly: keep the terminal operational and the lights lit. Outside, the "Fade" has consumed the stars, the cities, and hope. In the silence of the darkness, something stirs. Something that hates the light. If your generator fails, the game is over. But if you listen to the whispers in the radio static... it might already be too late.
